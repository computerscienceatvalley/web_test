Our Mission

Win From Within

Gatorade was born in a lab in 1965, when researchers at the University of Florida were asked to determine why so many players were being affected by heat and heat-related illnesses. The researchers discovered that the players were losing fluids and electrolytes through sweat, but weren't replacing them. They also determined that carbohydrates helped delay fatigue during exercise.

The result of this research was Gatorade, the first sports drink. Today, Gatorade continues to fuel athletes at all levels with scientifically formulated products to help them perform at their best.

Gatorade Sports Science Institute

Our commitment to research and innovation continues through the Gatorade Sports Science Institute (GSSI), which conducts research in exercise science, hydration and sports nutrition to help athletes optimize their health and performance.

About Gatorade

The Science of Sweat

Gatorade is more than just a thirst quencher. It's a scientifically formulated sports drink designed to help athletes hydrate better, perform stronger and recover faster. With the perfect balance of carbohydrates and electrolytes, Gatorade helps replace what athletes lose in sweat during intense training and competition.

From professional athletes to weekend warriors, Gatorade has been fueling performance for over 50 years. Our products are developed based on extensive research into how the body works during exercise and what it needs to perform at its best.

Our Products
